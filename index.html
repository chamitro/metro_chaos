Game Rules

Core Mechanics

  • Connect Stations: Click and drag between stations to create rail connections
  • Passengers: Appear at stations and need to reach their destination
  • Trains: Automatically pick up passengers and deliver them
  • Max Capacity: Each train holds 5 passengers maximum
  • Station Queue: Each station holds 15 passengers max (grace period allows more temporarily)

Money System

  • Starting Money: Each station starts with $1000
  • Money Drain: Active stations lose $2 per second
  • Earn Money: $5 per passenger delivered to a station
  • Station Closes: When money reaches $0
  • Reopening: Costs $200 per station

Factory System

  • Build Factories: Right-click empty areas, costs $200 (increases by $100 each)
  • Train Production: First train in 10 seconds, then 25 seconds each
  • Factory Capacity: Each factory can connect to 5 stations maximum
  • Refueling: Drag from factory to station to create fuel connection
  • Fuel Mechanic: Trains start with 100% fuel, lose 35% per station stop

Advanced Features

  • Merge Stations: Combine 3+ stations into mega hub, costs $500
  • Mega Stations: Higher capacity, can be refueled by factories
  • Grace Period: New stations get 8 seconds of unlimited capacity
  • Rush Hour: Occurs every 60 seconds, lasts 30 seconds, spawns 2.5x passengers
  • Combo System: Serving passengers quickly builds multiplier (+0.1 per serve, max 5.0x)
  • Efficiency: Based on queue usage - keep queues low for high efficiency

Fail Conditions

  • Station Overcrowded: Any station exceeds its capacity limit
  • Economic Collapse: 60% or more of stations close (run out of money)
  • Time Limit: (Story Mode only) Some levels have time limits
